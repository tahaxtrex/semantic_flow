MODULE RUBRICS
goal_focus

The content directly addresses the module's stated topic or heading
No significant off-topic digressions or filler paragraphs are present
Must-know concepts are prioritized over supplemental trivia
The opening of the segment makes the learning intent clear
The content density is appropriate — no padding, no repetition for the sake of length

text_readability

Sentences are reasonably short and not overloaded with clauses
Vocabulary matches the stated audience level
No grammatical errors or ambiguous phrasing detected
Ideas are presented one at a time rather than piled together
The text reads naturally without requiring re-reading to understand

pedagogical_clarity

Every technical term is defined at or before its first use
No unexplained acronyms or abbreviations are present
Sentence structure is appropriate for the intended audience
Terminology is used consistently throughout the segment
Explanations move from simple to complex, not the reverse

example_concreteness

At least one concrete example is present in the segment
Examples use realistic data or scenarios, not placeholders like a=5 or x=[1,2,3]
Each example demonstrates WHY the concept is useful, not just HOW to type it
Examples are complete enough to be followed without guessing
The example connects to a recognizable real-world situation

example_coherence

Examples use a consistent domain or theme within the segment
Later examples build on or reference earlier ones
Variable names and scenarios are consistent across examples
No two examples contradict each other
Examples collectively tell a coherent mini-story rather than being isolated snippets


COURSE RUBRICS
prerequisite_alignment

Foundational concepts are introduced before the modules that depend on them
The stated prerequisites in course metadata match what the content actually assumes
No module requires knowledge that was never taught earlier in the course
The course respects the target audience's expected starting knowledge level
Concept dependencies follow a clear logical order across modules

structural_usability

A clear table of contents exists and reflects the actual module structure
Module boundaries are clearly defined and consistently labeled
Navigation cues (headings, numbering, summaries) are present throughout
Technical requirements or setup instructions are easy to locate
The overall structure follows a recognizable and logical course format

business_relevance

The course connects its content to real professional tasks the target audience would face
At least some examples or exercises use industry-relevant scenarios
The course demonstrates WHERE and HOW skills are used in practice, not just what they are
Applied projects, case studies, or industry scenarios are present
A working professional in the target role would find the content practically useful

fluidity_continuity

Transitions between modules are explicit and intentional, not abrupt
Later modules reference and build on concepts from earlier ones
The course follows a single coherent narrative thread from start to finish
No module feels like it was inserted out of place
The pacing across modules is consistent — no sudden jumps in complexity

instructional_alignment

Every stated learning outcome is addressed by at least one module
The instructional depth matches the complexity demanded by the learning outcomes
Activities and exercises are aligned with what the learning outcomes require
The pacing is consistent with the stated audience level throughout
The course as a whole delivers on its stated objectives, not just touches them
